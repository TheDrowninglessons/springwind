About

Spring Wind: Where did it begin?

Founded in 1985, just a year after the horrible disaster that took place at 1428 Elm Street Los Angeles, CA.

Spring Wind set up their first Store Location at the residence on Elm Street, and have been serving the Local community/ abroad since. With the increase in reputation, the overwhelming amount of stock available for purchase, and the demand for our products in another location. We decided to increase our presence in our Local Continent, and expanded to Canada.

The expansion to Canada

Spring Wind decided to open up a second store and to expand into Canada. Spring Wind's second store is located at 562 Fairview Cr London, Ontario.

What currently is going on at Spring Wind?

Just recently Spring Wind bought the property at 112 Bark Street Glendora, CA. and started their third store!

Spring Wind would like to thank the local communities, partners and customers around the World. We are here because of you, and will grow to better serve you.
